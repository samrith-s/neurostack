## When to Use What

- **NeuroStack**: You want AI-powered knowledge management that runs entirely locally, with neuroscience-grounded features and zero vendor lock-in.
- **Obsidian**: You want a polished GUI editor with a large plugin ecosystem.
- **Khoj**: You want AI chat over your notes with cloud integration.
- **mem.ai**: You want automatic organization with minimal manual effort.
- **Notion AI**: You want AI features integrated into team collaboration.

NeuroStack complements Obsidian — use Obsidian as your editor, NeuroStack as your AI search and knowledge engine.
